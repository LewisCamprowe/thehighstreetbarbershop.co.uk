About

The High Street Barber Shop is a friendly professional traditional british barber shop that caters for the haircutting needs of boys and men of all ages. The owner has been working on the High Street since 2008 and endeavours to give complete satisfaction to each and every customer providing haircuts in a friendly, relaxing environment. The shop has two late opening evenings on Thursday and Friday (till 6:30pm) with street parking and a small car park off Mercer Street.
An appointments service is offered, with walk-ins available when time allows.
The barber shop sells products from American Crew, Tigi Bed Head for Men and The Mens Room Grooming range.
Check on Facebook for any announcements in any changes to shop times etc.
